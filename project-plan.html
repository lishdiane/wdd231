A Place to Eat

I chose this name because it represents the purpose of the site, a place to eat, and I think it is kind of catchy.

Site Purpose

This site will be a place that people can go to learn information about a local restaurant, and order food online.

Scenarios

  • What kind of food is offered at this establishment?
  • Do they have special offers?
  • Can I make an online order?
